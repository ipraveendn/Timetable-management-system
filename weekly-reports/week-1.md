 # Week 1 Report

Project Title: Timetable Management & Teacher Replacement System  
Week Duration: March 11 – March 18, 2026  

Work Done:
This week, we mainly focused on deciding the project idea. Initially, we came up with around five different problem statements and discussed each one in detail within the team. We analyzed their feasibility, usefulness, and how practical they would be to implement.

After that, we discussed these ideas with our project guide and took feedback. Based on the suggestions given, we refined our thinking and compared the ideas again. We also had discussions with mentors/directors to understand which idea would be more suitable and impactful.

Finally, we decided on the problem statement: "Timetable Management and Teacher Replacement based on availability and skill matching."

Once the problem statement was finalized, we created the GitHub repository for the project and prepared ourselves to start the development process from next week.
