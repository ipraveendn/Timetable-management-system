# Week 2 Report

Project Title: Timetable Management & Teacher Replacement System  
Week Duration: March 19 – March 26, 2026  

Work Done:
This week, we focused on validating our problem statement and understanding real-world requirements. We interacted with key stakeholders including HOD, Director, timetable coordinator, attendance coordinator, and multiple faculty members.

We presented our problem statement and proposed solution to them and received their feedback and approval. We also collected signatures from the concerned authorities for project acceptance.

Further, we had discussions with other teachers in the institution to understand additional challenges they face. Based on these interactions, we identified some additional requirements such as:
- Handling situations where a teacher arrives late and assigning a substitute temporarily  
- Managing weekly workload distribution among teachers  
- Handling room changes when classrooms are occupied (e.g., placement or training sessions) and notifying the respective teachers  

In addition to requirement gathering, we worked on preparing necessary documents and PPTs for the IA pitch scheduled this week.

We also explored and finalized the tools and technologies required for building the project. A small initial demo/prototype was created to understand the workflow.

We collected timetable data from the timetable coordinator, including current and previous semester schedules. We are yet to receive the full database from management, which is expected next week.
